History of DOGE

A DOGE is a viral meme featuring a Shiba Inu dog that became wildly popular on the Internet in 2013.

Where did the word DOGE come from? The misspelling of the word "dog" first circulated on the Internet around 2005.

Later, the word was simply picked up by various Internet users, and eventually, the word became associated with the Shiba Inu dog meme.
